About Molly

Molly V. Young is an accomplished nonprofit and small business leader and consultant, dedicated to taking initiatives from struggling to successful through strategic planning, human capital management and process improvement. Her experience in board governance, resource development, youth and adult learning, entrepreneurship and nonprofit management enables her to work with diverse entities to achieve diverse goals.

Molly holds a Bachelor’s degree in Political Science and Communications from DePauw University; a Master’s of Family, Youth, and Child Studies from the University of Nebraska; and an MBA from Colorado State University.

Her skills in hiring, managing and leading staff; ability to recognize problems and find creative solutions; experience improving processes and making them replicable for future success; innate gift in building and maintaining relationships; talent in written and verbal communication; and unparalleled level of integrity, come together to enable staff, clients, volunteers, and organizations to achieve goals.

Molly serves as a Founding Board Member of Global Sounds, a medical education nonprofit that provides medical resources and training to third world countries. She also serves on the Board of Morganizing for Change, a social entrepreneurship nonprofit for young people.

She resides in Washington, DC with her husband, Oliver, where they are referred to as “The Mollivers” by friends and family. In DC, they serve as foster parents through an organization called DC127.

Coaching

Motivating others to reach their highest potential takes one on one attention. Molly’s ability to recognize individuals’ strengths and help develop their skills is encouraging while enabling them to improve their effectiveness.

  • Provides coaching for Bittersweet Creative, a communications firm, working with staff to manage up, set and achieve goals, and learn to be their best selves.
  • Works with Maryland’s 24 Boards of Education to coach individual board members on communication, problem solving, and change management.

Event Planning

From conferences to board meetings, Molly is experienced in planning and coordinating special events. She helps you determine your goals for an event and executes the details to ensure a seamless experience.

  • Planned, coordinated, and executed three Board of Trustees meetings annually for National 4-H Council
  • Developed and implemented citizenship & leadership conference with over 4,500 annual participants at the National 4-H Youth Conference Center

Facilitation

People are your most valuable asset; investing in them is essential for growth and success. Molly designs and facilitates trainings, discussions, meetings, and workshops to help your team work with others, manage change, and lead well.

  • Wrote curriculum for and trained over 3,500 educators nationally on entrepeneurship through the Young Entrepeneur Foundation.
  • Leads workshops for government and non-profit professionals in leadership, management, and communications through Careerstone Group.

Resource Development

Telling your story and making the ask, is essential in obtaining additional resources. Molly’s expertise in fundraising, grant writing, proposal writing, business planning, and partnership development will support your growth in sales or fundraising.

  • Manages business development for Erin Scott Photography, a small business who has won best photographer in Washington, DC since 2001.

Strategic Planning

Thinking strategically is crucial to the growth of every organization and small business. Molly has the skills and experience to work with all stakeholders in designing your strategic plan and implementing a work plan to achieve goals.
